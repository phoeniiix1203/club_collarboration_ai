这是一个什么项目？

本项目改编自 arbitrary-image-stylization-tfjs.

Conventional style transfer solutions usually normally limited to a pre-selected handful of styles. In this project, two neural networks are used to provide infinitely many styles.

To be more specific, a style network first learns from any selected image and produce a 100-dimensional vector representing its style. This style vector is then fed into the transformer network, along with the content image, to produce the final stylized image.

Is my data safe? Can you see my pictures?

Both neural networks run in your browser. Your data and pictures never leave your computer!

How big are the models downloaded?

The distilled style network is ~9.6MB, while the separable convolution transformer network is ~2.4MB, for a total of ~12MB. Since these models work for any style, they only have to be downloaded once!

The image produced seems blurred/ugly/unsatisfactory?

Try again with different style image size and stylization strength. You can also use the "randomize parameters" button!
